Health & Wellness Programs

Comprehensive healthcare services and wellness programs designed to improve the overall well-being of women and their families in rural communities.

Our Health Services

Primary Healthcare

Access to basic healthcare services, regular check-ups, and essential medical care for women and children.

  • Regular health check-ups and screenings
  • Basic medical treatment and first aid
  • Vaccination programs for children
  • Health education workshops
  • Referral system for specialized care

Nutrition Education

Workshops and training on balanced diets, food preparation, and nutritional requirements for different age groups.

  • Balanced diet planning workshops
  • Local food resource utilization
  • Nutrition for pregnant women
  • Child nutrition programs
  • Food safety and hygiene training

Mental Wellness

Counseling services and mental health awareness programs to support emotional well-being and stress management.

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group therapy and support groups
  • Stress management workshops
  • Family counseling services
  • Mental health awareness campaigns
